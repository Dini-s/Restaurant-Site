our specials

Hot Garlic Roast Chicken

For Meat Lovers. Experience the irresistible flavors of our Hot Roasted Garlic Chicken. Each bite offers a mouthwatering explosion of savory goodness and a tantalizing aroma.

Hot Butter Cuttlefish

Our Signature Dish features golden, deep-fried, butter-coated cuttlefish garnished with spring onion and capsicum. Cooked in a savory hot butter sauce, this medium-spicy delight offers a perfect blend of rich flavors and tantalizing textures.

Sweet & Sour Prawns

Indulge in the perfect harmony of tangy and sweet with our Sweet & Sour Prawns. We serve the prawns and gravy separately, ensuring each bite is as fresh and flavorful as possible.

Our story

In the year 2001, two friends started a small eatery business as a partnership in Station Road Kandana offering Sri Lankan Food and Snacks. Growing steadily and learning the art of baking, in 2003 by acquiring modern baking facilities the business was converted into a bakery offering quality products and it was Renamed "Feastory Bakery".

Meet Our Chef

At the heart of our kitchen is passion, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence — all embodied by our head chef, Mr.Kodikara. With over 10 years of culinary experience, Chef Mr.Kodikara brings a unique blend of tradition and innovation to every dish. Trained in Hotel school in London, and inspired by flavors from around the world, he carefully crafts each plate to offer not just a meal, but a memorable dining experience. Chef Mr.Kodikara believes in using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that reflect both the season and the soul of the cuisine. From signature entrées to mouthwatering desserts, every element on your plate is a result of dedication, skill, and love for the culinary arts. Join us and discover the flavors crafted by a true artist in the kitchen.
